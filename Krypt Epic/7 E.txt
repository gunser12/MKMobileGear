Enigmatic Well of Souls
WEAPON

100% Chance to apply Curse on Combo Ender for 20sec.
[SHANG TSUNG] On tag-in Shang Tsung invokes the Well of Souls to the opponent. It applies a random debuff once in 10sec. The Well of Souls disappears on the opponent's tag-out.
[SHANG TSUNG] [SET] If this is equipped alongside Enigmatic Lantern, any DIAMOND Shang Tsung gains the ability to perform a Friendship. After 25 successful Critical Attacks, Power Drain applies to the opponent.
